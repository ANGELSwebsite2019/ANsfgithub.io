What is Plastic Pollution?

Plastic Pollution is an accumulation in the environment of synthetic plastic products to the point where they create problems for wildlife and their habitats as well for human populations.

What caused it?

On the go lifestyle requires plastic.

Trying to cut plastic usage is easier said than done, because once you wake up to plastic problem, you see it everywhere: in bags of grapes, in tubes of toothpaste and Tupperware containers, in bottles of dish soap and laundry detergent, in the wrappers of chips, the lining of milk cartons and even your trash can.

Letting go of these will take a lot of effort but it is not impossible.
